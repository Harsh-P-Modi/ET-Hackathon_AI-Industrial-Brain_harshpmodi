MAINTENANCE LOG — RECIPROCATING COMPRESSOR SYSTEM
Equipment: K-501 (Reciprocating Gas Compressor, 3-stage)
Location: Unit 200 — Hydrogen Recovery
Document: ML-K501-2024-Q1

=== EVENT 1 ===
Date: 2024-01-08
Work Order: WO-2024-0042
Type: Preventive Maintenance
Performed By: J. Martinez (Rotating Equipment Technician)
Description: Scheduled 6000-hour valve inspection on K-501 Stage 1 suction and discharge valves.
Findings: 
- Stage 1 suction valve (SV-501A) showed 15% seat erosion. Replaced with spare.
- Stage 1 discharge valve (DV-501A) within acceptable limits. No action required.
- Packing rings on rod seal replaced (normal wear).
- Lube oil sampled — no metal particles detected.
Downtime: 8 hours
Related Equipment: K-501, SV-501A, DV-501A, LO-P-501 (Lube Oil Pump)

=== EVENT 2 ===
Date: 2024-02-14
Work Order: WO-2024-0089
Type: Corrective Maintenance (Unplanned)
Performed By: R. Patel (Rotating Equipment Technician)
Description: K-501 tripped on high Stage 2 discharge temperature (TT-502 reading 185°C, alarm at 175°C).
Root Cause: Intercooler E-502 fouled — reduced heat transfer caused Stage 2 discharge to exceed limits.
Actions Taken:
- E-502 isolated and cleaned (hydroblasting). Found calcium carbonate scale buildup.
- Cooling water quality checked — pH was 8.9 (spec: 7.0-8.5). Water treatment adjusted.
- K-501 restarted after E-502 CIP. Discharge temp stable at 145°C.
Downtime: 36 hours (unplanned)
Related Equipment: K-501, E-502, TT-502, CW-System

=== EVENT 3 ===
Date: 2024-03-22
Work Order: WO-2024-0156
Type: Predictive Maintenance
Performed By: A. Singh (Vibration Analyst)
Description: Quarterly vibration analysis on K-501 crankshaft and crosshead bearings.
Findings:
- Overall vibration: 4.2 mm/s RMS (alarm: 7.1 mm/s) — within normal range.
- 1× RPM component dominant as expected for reciprocating machine.
- No sub-synchronous activity detected.
- Crosshead pin bearing shows early-stage wear signature (2× component increasing trend).
Recommendation: Schedule crosshead pin bearing replacement at next planned shutdown (estimated Q3 2024).
Downtime: 0 hours (online measurement)
Related Equipment: K-501, Crosshead-501

SYSTEM OVERVIEW:
K-501 compresses hydrogen-rich gas from 5 barg to 45 barg in 3 stages.
- Stage 1: 5 → 12 barg (intercooled by E-501)
- Stage 2: 12 → 28 barg (intercooled by E-502)
- Stage 3: 28 → 45 barg (aftercooled by E-503)
Lube oil system: LO-P-501 (main) / LO-P-502 (standby), LO-F-501 (duplex filter), LO-C-501 (cooler)
Anti-surge protection via recycle valve RV-501 (controlled by DCS compressor surge controller).
