COOLING WATER SYSTEM — P&ID DIAGRAM EXTRACT
Document: CWS-PID-001 Rev 3
Plant: Unit 400 — Ethylene Cracker Auxiliary Systems
Date: 2024-01-15

EQUIPMENT LIST:
- CW-P-101: Cooling Water Circulation Pump (Centrifugal, 500 GPM)
- CW-P-102: Cooling Water Standby Pump (Centrifugal, 500 GPM)
- CW-E-201: Shell & Tube Heat Exchanger (Duty: 2.5 MW)
- CW-E-202: Plate Heat Exchanger (Duty: 1.8 MW)
- CW-T-301: Cooling Tower Basin (Volume: 200 m³)
- CW-V-401: Expansion Vessel (Pressure rated: 6 barg)
- CW-FCV-101: Flow Control Valve (4", Pneumatic actuator)
- CW-TCV-201: Temperature Control Valve (6", Pneumatic actuator)
- CW-FT-101: Flow Transmitter (Electromagnetic, 4-20mA)
- CW-TT-201: Temperature Transmitter (RTD Pt100, 4-20mA)
- CW-PT-301: Pressure Transmitter (Gauge, 4-20mA)
- CW-LSH-301: Level Switch High (Float type)
- CW-LSL-301: Level Switch Low (Float type)

CONNECTIONS (PIPING):
CW-T-301 → CW-P-101 → CW-FCV-101 → CW-E-201 → CW-TCV-201 → CW-T-301
CW-T-301 → CW-P-102 → CW-FCV-101 (standby line)
CW-E-201 → CW-E-202 (series operation during peak load)
CW-V-401 connected to header between CW-P-101 and CW-FCV-101

INSTRUMENTATION NOTES:
- CW-FT-101 measures flow downstream of CW-P-101, input to DCS for cascade control of CW-FCV-101.
- CW-TT-201 measures outlet temperature of CW-E-201, controls CW-TCV-201 setpoint.
- CW-PT-301 monitors system header pressure. High alarm at 5.5 barg triggers CW-P-101 trip.
- CW-LSH-301 and CW-LSL-301 protect cooling tower basin from overflow and dry-run conditions.

SAFETY NOTES:
- If CW-P-101 trips on high pressure, CW-P-102 auto-starts within 5 seconds (permissive: CW-LSL-301 not active).
- Loss of cooling water flow >30 seconds triggers emergency shutdown of downstream process heat exchangers.
- CW-E-201 must be isolated before maintenance. Block valves XV-201A/B must be closed and locked out.
